Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Butterfly Effect (Netherlands) operates internal analytics tools that connect to social media platforms via authorized APIs. This policy explains what data these tools access, how it is stored, and who can see it.

2. Data we access

The tools connect to Butterfly Effect's own social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn) and retrieve aggregated metrics such as follower counts, impressions, reach, engagement, and public post metadata. No data of external users is accessed beyond what those platforms make available for the account owner.

3. How data is stored

Weekly snapshots are stored in a private GitHub repository accessible only to authorized Butterfly Effect team members. No data is shared with third parties outside of the platforms that originally provided it.

4. Data retention

Snapshots are retained indefinitely for historical trend analysis. You may request deletion by contacting stijn@butterflyeffect.nl.

5. API access tokens

Access tokens issued by the connected platforms are stored as encrypted secrets in GitHub Actions and are used solely to fetch analytics data. Tokens are rotated when they expire.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

As data subject you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data. Contact stijn@butterflyeffect.nl.
